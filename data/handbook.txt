COMPANY POLICY HANDBOOK 2025

1. REFUND POLICY
All refund requests must be submitted within 30 days of purchase. 
Refunds are processed via the original payment method. 
Please allow 5-10 business days for the funds to appear.

2. REMOTE WORK
Employees are allowed 2 days of remote work per week. 
Requests must be logged in the HR portal by Tuesday.

3. EXPENSES
Travel expenses under $50 do not require a receipt. 
Meals are capped at $25 per person.
